## Precedence

1. Unary operators (`+`, `-`, `~~~`, and `not`)
1. User-defined operators (including operators defined in the standard library such as `|>`)
1. Functions applied infix with backticks
1. Composition (`<+>`)
1. Multiplicative (`**`, `*`, and `/`)
1. Additive (`+` and `-`)
1. Shift (`<<<` and `>>>`)
1. Comparison (`<=`, `>=`, `<`, and `>`)
1. Equality (`==`, `!=`, and `<==>`)
1. Bitwise And (`&&&`)
1. Bitwise Xor (`^^^`)
1. Bitwise Or (`|||`)
1. Logical `and`
1. Logical `or`
1. Channel `<-`
